About Fresh

Fresh is a thriving internet station playing the latest and greatest hits. It was started originally in early 2019 by Bailey, but we have recently reopened in 2021.

Fresh aims to be an accepting community and online radio station playing all genres of music from dance to pop for you to enjoy. With staff from every corner of the world, all with different music tastes, there is always something at Fresh that fits your taste. Keep up with the Fresh community by joining our Discord server to talk with people and get the latest updates. No matter what makes you yourself, at Fresh we will accept you no matter what, we are a strong community ensuring everybody feels safe and comfortable when in our company, your comfort is our biggest priority.

Be sure to check out the timetable to check who's live when, or get your favourite song played on air using the request button on the right.
