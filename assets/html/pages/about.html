About Fresh

Fresh is a thriving internet station playing the latest and greatest hits. It started in early 2019 by Bailey, but recently reopened in 2020.

Fresh aims to be an accepting community which plays all genres of music for you to enjoy. With staff from everywhere across the world, all with different music tastes, there is always something at Fresh that fits your taste. Keep up with the Fresh community by joining our Discord server to talk with people and get the latest updates.

Be sure to check out the timetable to check who's live when, or get your favourite song played on air using the request button on the right.
